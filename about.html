About the Team

We’re a small team of IT students who came together with a graphic design student to build BAG for our capstone project. Three team members focused on programming, one focused on database work, and one helped shape the visual identity and branding. We’re all graduating soon, but we hope to keep improving BAG beyond this project.

Programming Student

Worked on application logic, core features, and front-end functionality across the BAG experience.

Team Member Two

Programming Student

Team Member Three

Programming Student

Team Member Four

Database Student

Team Member Five

Graphic Design Student

Created the visual direction for BAG, including branding, presentation, and the polished look of the project.

Our Goal

Why we built BAG

BAG was created to help students better understand, manage, and stretch their financial aid through the semester with a cleaner and more supportive experience.
